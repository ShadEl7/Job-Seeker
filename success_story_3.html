Overcoming Challenges: Peter's Career Pivot

Peter faced significant challenges when his industry was disrupted by technology. Here's how he successfully pivoted his career:

  • He identified transferable skills from his previous role, such as problem-solving and leadership.
  • Peter enrolled in a coding bootcamp to gain technical skills in demand.
  • He networked with professionals in the tech industry and secured an entry-level role as a software developer.

Peter's story shows that with the right mindset and effort, it's possible to overcome obstacles and start a new career path.
